Analysis

Like every Roulette system, you're going to lose 5.26% of all money wagered. There were some head scratching bets in this system - specifically, betting $5 on all three columns. This is a nonsense bet since collectively, the three bets together can never result in a win and will always lose on and . I have eliminated these three bets in my modified system.

Overall, this system has 7 good numbers, 7 bad numbers, and 24 near neutral numbers. The 7 whacks are going to kill you a huge amount of the time. On average, you'll get whacked every 5 to 6 spins. Since you're betting $235 per spin, you're going to lose about $12.36 per spin in the long run.
