--- layout: roulette-system title: Trips on the Outside - Roulette System Review ---

Betting system is the following:

  • Start with a blank spin until you hit a number 1-36.
  • Bet $10 on each of the two Dozens that didn't hit.
  • If you lose, Triple Martingale on the dozens ($30 each) and add $5 on the 0-00 Split
  • If you lose, bump the dozens to $100 each and bet $15 on the 0-00 Split
  • Reset to initial bet on win.
